PATCHLOG ROADMAP
================

Reliable core
-------------

- Keep planning side-effect-free and apply mutations only after review and
  grounded policy checks.
- Keep version bumps transactional and Git staging limited to the exact files
  returned by the bump plan.
- Preserve dry-run immutability with filesystem and Git-state E2E tests.

Grounded release signals
------------------------

- Import true code coverage from CI coverage artifacts.
- Build dependency risk from language-aware dependency graphs.
- Derive PR lead time and deployment cycle time from PR, merge, deployment,
  and production timestamps.
- Keep touched-test-file ratio, change-complexity proxy, cross-cutting-change
  risk, contribution concentration, commit span, and release age descriptive
  only. Never use these proxies as release gates.

Advanced workflows
------------------

- Continue moving provider publication, Confluence, trend dashboards, drift,
  and curation behind focused subcommands and explicit interfaces.
- Keep DPI, team-health scoring, and gamification opt-in under labs until their
  validity, privacy impact, and organizational use are independently reviewed.
